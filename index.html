Private Business Corporation(PBC)

PBC company is ideal for Small Medium Enterprises(SMEs). According to Zimra, SMEs falls into a range, classified by having employees below 75 and an annual turnover not exceeding $1 000 000. A PBC restricts ownership from 1 to 20 shareholders. You can operate your sole trader business without any fear of loosing control. Different types certificates are awarded when you register as a PBC or PLC company. With PBC you get a Statement & Certificate of Incorporation which summarizes all your company information on one paper.
